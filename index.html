Semiconductor Wafer Map Defect Classification using Transfer Learning

Semiconductor chip manufacturing is costly and time-consuming, with testing being a major contributor to production costs. Detecting and labeling wafer map defects during manufacturing is crucial for identifying their causes. In this paper, we compare various transfer learning methods, including VGG19, MobileNet, ResNet, and DenseNet, for classifying wafer map defects using the WM811K dataset. We consolidated the eight defect classes into four, achieving a highest accuracy of 95.56% with VGG19.
